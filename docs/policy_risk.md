# Security Policy & Risk Management

## Overview

This course focuses on applying structured approaches to identifying, assessing, and managing cybersecurity risks at the organizational level.

## Objective

- Understand risk management principles  
- Apply security frameworks such as NIST CSF  
- Evaluate organizational security posture  
- Develop incident response and mitigation strategies  

## Key Activities

- Conducted risk assessments to identify vulnerabilities and threats  
- Applied NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) to analyze security maturity  
- Evaluated governance, compliance, and policy requirements  
- Developed incident response and risk mitigation strategies  

## Skills Developed

- Risk assessment and analysis  
- Security frameworks (NIST CSF)  
- Policy and governance understanding  
- Incident response planning
